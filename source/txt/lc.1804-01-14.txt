January 14, 1804Clark
Saturday 14th January    a Snow fall last night of about an Inch [and one] half    The river <Still riseing> falling and running with Ice, a fair Sun shineing morning—    the party Caught 14 Rabits to day & 7 yesterday.    a Cold afternoon. The Mississippi, is Closed with Ice.

Probably eastern cottontails, Sylvilagus floridanus. Hall, 1:300.
