November 23, 1804Clark23rd    a fair warm Day, wind from the S. E.    Send after Stone    Several men with bad Colds, one man Sheilds with the Rhumitism    the river on a Stand haveing rose 4 Inches in all
OrdwayFriday 23rd Nov    pleasant & warm    the pearogue Sent for Stone. Rope works fixed. Several hands employed makeing a large Rope for the purpose of drawing the Barge up the Bank &.C.
