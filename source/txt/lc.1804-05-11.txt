May 11, 1804Clark
Friday 11th    a warm morning    I write all day    in the evengn. about 4 oClock a violent gust from the N W by W, [rasd?] the [vestles?] up the Creek Some distance, Seven french arrive with Drewyer—    I send Drewyer and the horse's to St LouisThese "french" may have been boatmen recruited by Drouillard in the river settlements.
