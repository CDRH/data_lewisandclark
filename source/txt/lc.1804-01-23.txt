January 23, 1804Clark
Smunday [Monday] 23rd Jany 1804    a Cloudy morning but little Ice runig to day    The Thermtr. at 8 oClock 11° abo 0, at 12 oClock Stood at 10° abov 0, (I pen an Ordr to Corpl.)    at 3 oClock 17° abo 0, in the evening the wind raised [receed?] and Shifted to the North    Cought 14 rabits & Killed 2 Deer—
