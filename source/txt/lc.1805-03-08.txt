March 8, 1805Clark8th of March Friday 1805
a fair morning Cold and windey, wind from the East, visited by the Greesey head & a Riarca to day, those men gave Some account of the Indians near the rockey mountains
a young Indian same nation & Different Village Stole the Doughter of the Black man [NB: Mandan (Minetarie], he went to his Village took his horse & returned & took away his doughter
OrdwayFriday 8th March 1805. Some cloudy & cold.    a nomber of the Savages continue bringing corn and fat dryed buffaloe meat for to Git Blacksmiths work done.
