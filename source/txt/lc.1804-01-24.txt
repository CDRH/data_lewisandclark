January 24, 1804Clark
Tuesday 24th    a Butifull morning    Clear Sunshine    the wi[n]ds ligh[t] from the N W, the Thermometer at 9 oClock Stood at 4° abo 0, (I take fesick), at 10 oClock 8° abov 0, wind West, at 12 oClock—14° abo 0, at 3 oClock 11° abov 0, Smone [some] Small pieces of Ice running    Sick, [games?] to day

"Physic," probably a laxative, the standard remedy for nearly every ailment at the time.
