January 18, 1804Clark
Wednesday 18th Jany.    a Cloudy morning with moderate breaze from the N W. b[y] W    The river run with Ice, at 8 oClock the Thromtr. Stood at 1° below 0,—    9 oClock 1° ab[ove] 0, at 10 oClock 2° abov 0, at 11 oClock rose to 4° above 0, and beg[an] to Snow, at 12 oClock The Thermt. at 2 above 0. Snow above 1 Inch    at 1 oClock 2 abov 0, at 2 oClock 1 abov 0, and left off Snowing—
