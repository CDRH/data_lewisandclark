January 15-16, 1806GassThe 15th and 16th were both wet throughout, and the men employed as on the 14th. In the morning of the 17th there were some clouds; but about 10 o'clock they disapeared and we had a fine day.— About the same time 8 of the natives of the Clatsop nation came to our fort, and stayed till the evening. A hunter went out in the morning and killed a deer.
Whitehouse
Wednesday 15th Thursday 16 January    It rained hard, & we had stormey Weather during these two days


Including Chief Coboway.Colter.
