January 27, 1804Clark
Friday 27th Jany: 1804.    a Cloudy morning    Some Snow, this morning I am verry unwell, to day at 1 oClock 28 abov 0, (I send off Howrd express to day to Cap Lewis at Koho: [Cahokia] with a Letter <I am some better,>    Winser who was out Last night returned    he Killed a Deer & Turkey—    Gibson Killed 1 Deer to day, Collons one near Camp
