December 3, 1804Clark3rd December Monday 1804.
a fine morning    the after part of the day Cold & windey    the wind from the N W.    The Father of the Mandan who was killed Came and made us a present of Some Dried Simnens [NB: Pumpkins] & a little pemicon [NB: pemitigon], we made him Some Small preasents for which he was much pleased
OrdwayMonday 3rd Decr.    cold & windy.    the work carried on as usal.    commenced Setting up the pickets and bring pickets &.C. &.C.
