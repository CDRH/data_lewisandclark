March 14, 1805Clark14th March Thursday 1805.
a fine day    Set all hands to Shelling Corn &c.    Mr. McKinsey leave us to day    maney Indians as usial.    wind west    river Still riseing
OrdwayThursday 14th March 1805.    clear & warm. Mr. Sharbono a frenchman who we expected would go with us has lately too[k] another notion and has pitched a lodge outside of the Garrison and moved out.    Mr Gravelleen has joined in his place.—
