February 6, 1804Clark
Monday 6th Feby    a fair day    Snow nearly gone, Some Ice Still running    Sick take Walnut pills, Winser killed a Badger. My P[ills]—work &c.    great numbers of wild fowl flying Notherly. Swans in great number, river began to fall    Thompson return from KohokiaTaxidea taxus. See entries and note at July 30, 1804. Cutright (LCPN), 70; Hall, 2:1009–13.
