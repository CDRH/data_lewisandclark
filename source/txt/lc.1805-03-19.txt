March 19, 1805Clark19th of March 1805    Cold windey Day Cloudy    Some little Snow last night    Visited to Day by the big white & Little Crow, also a man & his wife with a Sick Child, I administer for the child    I am told that two parties are gorn to war from the Big bellies and one other party going to war Shortly.
OrdwayTuesday 19th March 1805.    cloudy    a light Squawl of Snow.    cold air.    about 10 oClock Sergt. Gass came down to the Fort and informed us that the perogues were finished, and more men wanting to draw them to the River which is about one mile & a half.
