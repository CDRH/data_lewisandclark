December 29, 1803Clark
29th    Snow this morning    Cloudey & wet all day, finish my hut and write 2 Letters one to G. H. & one to Col Anderson    rain at night

	
Perhaps George Hancock of Fincastle, Virginia, whose daughter Julia married Clark in 1808. Osgood (FN), 10 n. 6.Richard Clough Anderson, a Virginian, was a soldier in the American Revolution. Moving to Kentucky in 1783, he married a sister of Clark's.
