February 4, 1804Clark
Satturday [written over another word, perhaps "effect"] 4    Discharge Leakens for theift with a Small Correction    a warm Day    Some rain last night, in the Evening the River Covered with large Sheetes of Ice from both rivers, the River & Creek rised Suffecent to take the boat up the Creek some distance    moderate day    I am verry Sick    wild fowl pass

The river was the Mississippi and the creek River Dubois; see below, February 7, 1804.
