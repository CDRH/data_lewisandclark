March 5, 1805

[Clark]

5th March Tuesday 1805
 

A fine Day    Themometer at 40° abo 0.    Several Indians visit us to day    one frenchman cross to join a Indian    the two pass through by Land to the Ricaras with a Letter to Mr. Tabbow

[Ordway]

 

Tuesday 5th March 1805.    clear and pleasant.    the men at work making coal &.C.    a light Squawl of Snow fell about 4 oClock this morning.    nothing extroardinary.
