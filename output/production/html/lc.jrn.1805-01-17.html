January 17, 1805

[Clark]

17th January Thursday 1805
 

a verry windey morning hard from the North    Thermometer at 0, Several Indians here to day

[Ordway]

 

Thursday 17th Jany.    a clear cold morning.    the wind high from the N. W.

[Gass]

 

On the 17th it became cold; the wind blew hard from the north, and it began to freeze.

[Whitehouse]

 

Thursday 17th Jany. 1805.    about 3 oC. this morning the wind began to blow from the North & began to freeze.    continued cold & the wind the Same course all day.

Thursday January 17th    This morning about 3 o'Clock the Wind began to blow from the North, & freeze which continued all this day, the Weather being very Cold—
