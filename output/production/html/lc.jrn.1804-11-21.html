November 21, 1804

[Clark]

 

21st Novr. Wednesday    a fine Day    dispatched a perogu and Collected Stone for our Chimnys, Some wind from the S. W.    arrange our different articles—    maney Indians visit us to day, G D [Drouillard] hurd his hand verry bad—    all the party in high Spirits—    The river Clear of ice, & riseing a little—

[Ordway]

 

Wednesday 21st Nov.    cloudy & warm    1 of the pearogues Sent a Short distance down the River for Stone for the Back of our chimneys    4 backs made &.C
