May 11, 1804

[Clark]

 

Friday 11th    a warm morning    I write all day    in the evengn. about 4 oClock a violent gust from the N W by W, [rasd?] the [vestles?] up the Creek Some distance, Seven french arrive with Drewyer— [1]    I send Drewyer and the horse's to St Louis

1. These "french" may have been boatmen recruited by Drouillard in the river settlements. (back)
