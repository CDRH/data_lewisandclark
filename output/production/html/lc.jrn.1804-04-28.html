April 28, 1804

[Clark]

 

Saturday 28    Mr. Hay packing up    all hands at work prepareing. Several Country men Came to win my mens money, in doing So lost all they had, with them.    river fall    Say on [1]

1. These last two words appear at the bottom of document 11 and at the top of document 12 of the Field Notes, apparently as a guide to continuity. (back)
