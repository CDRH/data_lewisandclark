January 24, 1805

[Clark]

24th January Thursday 1805
 

a fine day, our inturpeters appear to understand each others better than a fiew days past    Sent out Several hunters, they returned without killing any thing, Cut Coal wood—

[Ordway]

 

Thursday 24th Jany.    colder this morning than it has ben for Several days past.    in the afternoon five men employed cutting coal wood as our coal was jest Gone.

[Gass]

 

Thursday 24th.    A cold day. Some of our hunters went out, but killed nothing.
