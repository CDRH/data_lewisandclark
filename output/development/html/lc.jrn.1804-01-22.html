January 22, 1804

[Clark]

 

Sunday 22nd Jany: 1804    Snow all the last night, and Snows this morning, the debth is 5¾ Inches, The Thermometer Stands at 9 oClock this morning in the Open Air at 11° abov 0,—, at 12 oClock rose to 14° abov 0, and Stoped Snowing    wind Easterly    at 3 oClock 13d abv 0, riv nearly Clear of Ice,
