January 27, 1804

[Clark]

 

Friday 27th Jany: 1804. [1]    a Cloudy morning    Some Snow, this morning I am verry unwell, to day at 1 oClock 28 abov 0, (I send off Howrd express to day to Cap Lewis at Koho: [Cahokia] with a Letter [2] 〈I am some better,〉    Winser who was out Last night returned    he Killed a Deer & Turkey—    Gibson Killed 1 Deer to day, Collons one near Camp

1. Under the beginning of this entry is the following calculation, apparently from an astronomical observation:
