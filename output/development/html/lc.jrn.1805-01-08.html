January 8, 1805

[Clark]

8th of January Tuesday 1805
 

a Cold Day    but fiew indians at the fort to day    wind from the N, W, one man at the Village [1]

[Ordway]

 

Tuesday 8th Jany. I went up to the village    the wind blew cold from N. W.

1. Ordway says it was himself in his entry for this day. (back)
