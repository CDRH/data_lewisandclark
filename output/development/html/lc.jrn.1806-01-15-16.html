January 15-16, 1806

[Gass]

 

The 15th and 16th were both wet throughout, and the men employed as on the 14th. In the morning of the 17th there were some clouds; but about 10 o'clock they disapeared and we had a fine day.— About the same time 8 of the natives of the Clatsop nation [1] came to our fort, and stayed till the evening. A hunter [2] went out in the morning and killed a deer.

[Whitehouse]

 

Wednesday 15th Thursday 16 January    It rained hard, & we had stormey Weather during these two days

1. Including Chief Coboway. (back)
2. Colter. (back)
